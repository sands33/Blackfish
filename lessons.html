We are mobile outfit, giving you access to a variety of great places to paddle in the region. Depending on weather, water conditions, and your preferences, we have a number of options to choose from in both salt and freshwater.

Lessons

Learn how to SUP from a professional. Taking a lesson with Blackfish is a great way to learn in the tradition of the Hawaiian watermen/women, who the sport is back traced to. We will cover safety concerns, proper technique, different paddle strokes, turning, balance, navigating waves and boat wakes, and more. First time? Tenth time? Wherever you are with stand up paddleboarding, our lessons are not only instructive but also a ton of fun!

  • 2.5 hour: $69

Learn to Race

Know how to paddle and want to take it to the next level? Let Blackfish SUP introduce you to the competitive side of the sport. You will boost your confidence learning racing stokes, the buoy turn, drafting, start line strategy, and more. Taught by an experienced Pacific Northwest SUP competitor and water athlete. This class is offered either as a one off session or on a weekly basis.

  • 2 hours: $59 (one session)
  • Weekly (4 weeks): $199
