FAQ

I have bad balance. Can I still successfully paddle a SUP?

  • YES! The boards we use are stable and very beginner friendly. And falling in is the most fun part anyway! So give it a whirl, you will not be disappointed.

My abs are not that strong and I have heard SUP is all about the abs. Can I still learn to SUP?

  • For sure! All the talk about needing stand up paddleboarding and abdominal strength comes from the fact that once you are at an intermediate level, engaging the core is a key part of the technique, resulting in strengthening of the core. But, you do not need abs of steel to learn how to SUP!

I tried SUPing in Hawaii. Is it not too cold for SUPing in Washington?

  • Not with the right gear. We paddle all year round, whether it is a sultry 85 degree day in July or a frigid 20 degree day in January. The proper gear makes all the difference, and we have everything you need, including wetsuits, booties, gloves, etc.
