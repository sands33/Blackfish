Who We Are

Ryan Kuja, Owner/Instructor is an all around waterman whose lifelong connection to the ocean began through surfing in New England, Ryan has lived and surfed in South Africa, Mozambique, Nicaragua, El Salvador, Haiti and beyond. He has been paddling the waters of the Pacific Northwest for several years, racing and exploring the hidden reaches of the region's waterways. Ryan is certified as a stand up paddleboard instructor through the Professional Stand Up Paddle Association (PSUPA) and is a Safe SUP Ambassador for MTI Adventurewear, and is CPR/First Aid certified. He also recently completed a master's degree at The Seattle School of Theology and Psychology.

Katie Kuja, Co-owner/Instructor grew up on the beaches of Michigan, but Katie did not fully discover her love of the water until she met and married her waterman husband, Ryan. She has since grown to love exploring all the Pacific Northwest has to offer via stand up paddleboarding. Besides being seen on her SUP, Katie can also be seen running, cycling, attempting to surf or just out enjoying this beautiful area with good company. Katie is an occupational therapist and practices in Aberdeen. CPR/First Aid certified.
